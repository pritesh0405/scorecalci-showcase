PrivacyPolicy

We built ScoreCalci to be a private, offline-first experience. This policy explains exactly what data we touch — and what we don't.

Effective  June 11, 2026   |   App  ScoreCalci v1.0   |   Platform  Android
01

Overview

ScoreCalci is a sports scoring and tournament management application. We collect only the data you actively create inside the app. We do not collect any personal, behavioural, or device-level data.

🔒

No account creation required. No email. No phone number. Authentication is fully anonymous.

02

Data We Collect

ScoreCalci collects only the data you create within the app:

  • Match records — scores, sport type, date, players or teams you enter, and match duration.
  • Tournament records — bracket data, round results, and tournament settings you create.
  • Player names — names you enter for saved players. No profile photos or contact details.
  • App preferences — theme and display settings stored locally on your device.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, payment information, browsing history, or device identifiers.

03

Authentication

ScoreCalci uses anonymous authentication via Firebase Auth. A random, non-identifiable user ID is generated automatically when you first open the app.

👤

No registration, login form, or personal credentials are required or stored. Your anonymous UID cannot be traced back to you as an individual.

04

How Data Is Stored

Your data lives in two places depending on its type:

Data Type Description Location
Match records Scores, players, sport, result Both
Tournament records Brackets, rounds, progression Both
Player names Saved player profiles Device
App preferences Theme, display settings Device
Anonymous UID Firebase Auth identifier Cloud

Device storage uses Android SharedPreferences. Cloud storage uses Google Cloud Firestore, scoped strictly to your anonymous user ID.

05

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your data with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Your data is stored in Google Firebase infrastructure and is therefore also governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Firebase is used solely for anonymous authentication and cloud sync — not analytics or advertising.

06

Data Deletion

You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time:

  • Delete match history from within the app via Settings → Data & Backup → Clear All Match History.
  • Uninstalling ScoreCalci removes all locally stored data immediately.
  • Cloud data is deleted within 30 days of an account deletion request.

07

Children's Privacy

ScoreCalci is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through our app, contact us and we will remove it promptly.

08
