What We Collect

ScoreCalci collects only the data you create within the app. We do not collect personal identifiers, location, or payment information.

Category Type Storage
IdentityAnonymous Firebase UIDCloud
Match DataScores, sport, players, duration, resultDevice + Cloud

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, location, or any personally identifiable information. Authentication is fully anonymous.

02
How to Delete

You can delete all your data directly from within the app at any time — no contact required.

1

Open Settings

Tap the settings icon on the main screen of ScoreCalci.

2

Go to Data & Backup

Select Data & Backup from the settings menu.

3

Clear Match History

Tap Clear All Match History under the Danger Zone section to remove match history stored by the app. If cloud data remains associated with your anonymous account, you may request deletion by email.

4

Uninstall the App

Uninstalling ScoreCalci removes all locally stored data — players, tournaments, and preferences — immediately.

03
Email Request

Prefer to contact us directly? Send a deletion request and we'll process it within 30 days and confirm by email.
