02

How to Delete Your Data

You can delete all your data directly from within the app at any time, without contacting us.

1

Open Settings

Tap the settings icon on the main screen of ScoreCalci.

2

Go to Data & Backup

Select Data & Backup from the settings menu.

3

Clear Match History

Tap Clear All Match History under the Danger Zone section to remove all local and cloud match data.

4

Uninstall the App

Uninstalling ScoreCalci removes all locally stored data (players, tournaments, preferences) from your device immediately.

⚠ Alternative method

Request Deletion by Email

If you prefer, you can contact us directly to request deletion of your cloud data. We will process your request within 30 days and send confirmation.
